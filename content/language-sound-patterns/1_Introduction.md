## Phonetics (Part 1)

Articulatory phonetics:
- how speech sounds are produced in mouth
- basis of phonology

Acoustic phonetics:
- study of acoustic signals
- spectrograms

Auditory phonetics:
- studying of hearing and perception
- ear and brain
